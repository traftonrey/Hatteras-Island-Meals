Our Mission:

To serve anyone on Hatteras Island who is unable to prepare nutritious meals, has no one to help with meal preparation, requires short term assistance while recovering from an illness or medical condition or needs long term meals.

What We Do

Hatteras Island Meals provides nutritious meals for lunch Monday through Friday to elderly or homebound clients on Hatteras Island.

HIM is an all-volunteer organization. We work together to enhance the quality of life for our recipients. Through meal delivery and regular contact with our caring volunteers, we positively impact health, independence, and connectivity.

Follow us on Facebook for regular updates on how we serve our community.

We need your help.

HIM delivers nearly 9,000 meals anually. The cost to feed one recipient for a week is $35. Every donation counts and helps us fulfill our mission.
