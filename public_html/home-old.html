Our Mission

To serve anyone on Hatteras Island who is unable to prepare nutritious meals, who has no one to help with meal preparation, who requires short term assistance while recovering from an illness or medical condition, or requires long term meals. Hatteras Island Meals, Inc. is a nonprofit 501(c) organization.
